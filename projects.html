Authors: Christian Crouzet, Danny F. Xie, Maiella Nona L. Nuqui, Jonathan Hasselman, Thinh Phan, Robert H. Wilson, David Baglietto-Vargas, Celia Da Cunha, Hayk Davtyan, Stefania Forner, Amandine Jullienne, Afsheen Bazrafkan, Frank M. LaFerla, Andre Obenaus, Mathew Blurton-Jones, Yama Akbari, Kim N. SPIE / Neurophotonics • 2025

Investigates structural and functional cerebrovascular changes in a mouse model of late-onset Alzheimer’s disease, with the goal of understanding early vascular contributions to disease progression. I contributed to the project by designing and refining key data visualizations that helped illustrate the imaging results and vascular metrics presented in the paper.
